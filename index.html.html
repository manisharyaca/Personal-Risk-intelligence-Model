Step 1 — capacity before appetite

Can you actually survive this?

Appetite is what you choose to risk. Capacity is what you can survive. Most blow-ups aren't a bad trade — they're trading money you needed. Capacity is the gate; nothing downstream fixes a breach here.

Not your net worth. Money whose loss changes nothing about your life.
